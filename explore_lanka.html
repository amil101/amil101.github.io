TThis nine night/ten days tour has been designed to reveal an insight to the cultural heritage of Sri Lanka and perfect opportunity to experience the cool hills and sunny beaches wild safaris with adventures fun and relax journey of Sri Lanka in luxury.

Journey
Day 1 : Negombo

Negombo is a city on the west coast of Sri Lanka, north of the capital, Colombo. Near the waterfront, the remains of the 17th-century Dutch Fort now house a prison. Negombo Lagoon, lined with fishermen’s huts, feeds into the Dutch-era Hamilton Canal. The canal leads south to Colombo. Neoclassical St. Mary’s Church, completed in the 1920s, features a ceiling decorated with vivid religious paintings.

  • - Arrival to Sri Lanka (Bandaranayaka International Airport) in Katunayaka
  • - Proceed to Hotel
  • - Check-in to the hotel & relax
  • - Evening proceed to Negombo Church and Beach
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 2 : Dambulla

The Dambulla city is located in the central province of the Sri Lanka and it comes with several beautiful and unique attractions like the Dambulla Cave Temple Complex. Apart from that, the 14 metre Buddha Statue that is craved out from the stone as well as several ancient and well preserved statues will live you breathless. Besides, Dambulla is also very popular for being home to the biggest rose quartz mountain range in the whole Asia as well as the NamalUyana, the iron wood forest.The other side of the island has the great rock fortress of Sigiriya, which is another beautiful and attractive tourist spot that you can visit.

  • - Proceed to Hotel in Habarana
  • - En-rout and visit to Dambulla cave temple (Golden Temple & Golden Buddha Statue)
  • - Check-in to the hotel & relax
  • - Evening proceed to Sigiriya
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 3 : Kandy

The cultural capital of Sri Lanka, the Kandy city is full of history, culture and unique personality. The city is surrounded by some majestic mountains and it attractive touring destinations for the travelers, who want to enjoy the natural beauty of this island along with exploring the rich and colorful culture. This city is also situated very close to the Pinnawala. Elephant Orphanage and visiting this place will be a unique experience for the travelers. Because this place is the only place in the whole world where you can help the elephants for bathing and feed along with this, you can even get engaged in working for their rehabilitation. Check in to the hotel in Dambulla and overnight stay.

  • - En route, visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
  • - Proceed to Kandy city
  • - Kandy city tour (Upper lake drive, Gem & Bathik shopping, Oak Ray wood carvings)
  • - Witness a cultural dance in the evening
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 4 : Nuwara Eliya

The city of Nuwara Eliya is also known as the 'Little England', for the weather it has as well as the amazing colonial architecture and buildings which are seen all over the city.This hill town has plenty to offer you that surely leave you breathless. This is just some of the marvelous places you can visit in the event you decide to stay here.

  • - After breakfast at the hotel, visit Temple of Tooth & participate in the morning ceremony
  • - Proceed to Nuwaraeliya known as Little England
  • - En route, Tea Plantation & Tea Factory visit
  • - Visit beautiful waterfalls in Hill country
  • - Take Nuwaraeliya city Tour (Strawberry farm visit, boat ride can be arranged)
  • - Check in to the hotel and overnight Stay
Day 5 : Yala

The Yala National Park is the most popular wildlife safari in the whole Sri Lanka. This park is renowned for the home to plenty of endemic and non-endemic animals as well as the diverse plants. In spite of their elusive nature, the majority of the visitors will certainly locate leopard in the recreation centre, because of the high number of leopard lives in this park. You can experience this amazing park in a jeep safari escorted by knowledgeable and learned guides and drivers and explore some astonishing animals that live here.

  • - After Breakfast at the hotel proceed to Yala
  • - Tour and Visit Ella
  • - Take a 4x4 Jeep safari in Yala National Park in the afternoon or next day early morning
  • - Check in to the hotel & relax
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 6 : Deniyaya

Deniyaya is a small town in Sri Lanka. It is located in Matara District of the Southern Province which is very near to Sinharaja Rain Forest.Sinharaja Forest Reserve is a national park and a biodiversity hotspot in Sri Lanka. It is of international significance and has been designated a Biosphere Reserve and World Heritage Site by UNESCO.

  • - After Breakfast at the hotel proceed to Deniyaya.
  • - Tour and Visit Walawwa
  • - Take in Sinharaja Rain Forest in the next day early morning
  • - Check in to the hotel & relax
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 7 : Benthota

The amazing Bentota is situated 200 km away from the Equator and very near to the Southern tip of the Island of Sri Lanka. The eye-catching beauty of this bright and sandy beach will transform your dreams and visions of a tropical heaven into an everyday reality. This breath-taking beach is crescent shaped and it plenty of natural beauties to offer you as this is an ideal place for just sitting and relaxing. And you will forget about the rusty life that is far away from you.

  • - After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Bentota
  • - Galle City tour (2 hours)
  • - Engage in water sports
  • - Evening enjoy the sun set and relax at the beach
  • - overnight stay at the Hotel
Day 8 : Benthota

The beautiful Bentota is located very close to the southern tip of the Sri Lanka Island and just 200 km away from the Equator. The breath-taking beauty of this golden and bright sandy beach will turn your dreams of a tropical heaven into a regular reality.This place is the ideal place for just to sit and relax along with enjoy the warm sand of the beaches, as you will definitely forget about the hastiness and rustiness of your other life that is far away from you.

  • - After Breakfast at the hotel leisure & relax in the beach
  • - Boat Safari.
  • - Visit Ayurvadic Garden and Fish Theropy
  • - Overnight stay at the hotel
Day 9 : Colombo

The city of Colombo is the commercial capital of Sri Lanka and it holds together the new and old trend of the county very beautifully. The city also represents the colonial remnants with high-rise hotels and commercial banks. Additionally, Colombo was the eighth-century port and it houses several financial, administrative, and other major institutions that are of relevance to the travelers. Right now, the city has been developed into a modern city and boasts the amazing blend of Sri Lanka's ethnic race and religious people.

  • - After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Colombo
  • - On the way you get to visit a turtles hatchery in Kosgoda
  • - Check in to the hotel & relax at the hotel
  • - Later proceed with a Colombo Shopping and City Tour
Day 10 : Departure

Breakfast at the hotel and transfer to Airport for the departure flight.
